Essential Variables for Peer-to-Peer Analysis in Different Industries

Key Metrics and Insights for Comparative Sector Analysis

                    When analyzing companies within the same sector, focusing on specific variables can provide clearer insights. Here's a breakdown of the essential variables for peer-to-peer 
                    analysis across various industries.

                    
1. Agrochemical
                    Specialty vs Generic PF (Product Form) + Margins + Formulations vs Technical Manufacturer + Volume Growth + New Product Launches
                    
                    
2. Airlines
                      Market Share + Debt + CASK (Cost per Available Seat Kilometer)
                    
                    
3. Auto ANCs (Auto Ancillaries)
                      Which Underlying Auto are you exposed to + Possibility of operating leverage
                    
                    
4. Banks
                      NIMS (Net Interest Margins) + ROA (Return on Assets) + GNPAs (Gross Non-Performing Assets) + ROE's (Return on Equity) + Management + Loan Growth
                    
                    
5. Brokers
                      Market Share of Brokers
                    
                    
6. Capital Goods
                     Order Book + Operating Leverage + End User Industry Capex (Capital Expenditure)
                    
                    
7. Cement
                      EV (Enterprise Value)/Per tonne + Per KG (Kilogram) realizations + Regional or National Player
                    
                    
8. CDMO (Contract Development and Manufacturing Organization)
                     Patented vs Non Patented PF (Product Form) + Phase 3 + Commercial Projects + Margins
                    
                    
9. Chemicals
                    Cash Conversion vs Chemistry + Leadership vs Margins
                    
                    
10. Convertors
                     Getting Into Stable/Higher Margin Business
                    
                    
11. Defence
                     Order book + Bid Pipeline
                    
                    
12. Domestic Pharma
                     Chronic vs Acute + Margins + MNC (Multinational Corporation) + Market Leadership + MR (Medical Representative) Productivity
                    
                    
13. Exchanges
                     Volume Growth + New Product Launches
                    
                    
14. FMCG Companies (Fast-Moving Consumer Goods)
                     Volume Growth + Category Under Penetration
                    
                    
15. Food Delivery
                    AOV (Average Order Value) + Contribution Margin + Take Rates + EBITDA (Earnings Before Interest, Taxes, Depreciation, and Amortization) Margins + Order Growth 
                    + Share
                    
                    
16. Hospitals
                     Margin Comparison + Growth Rates
                    
                    
17. Hotels
                     Expansion Plans + Margins + REV PAR (Revenue Per Available Room) + Luxury vs Mid Premium?
                    
                    
18. Infra Companies
                     Order Wins + Margins + D/E (Debt to Equity Ratio)
                    
                    
19. Microfinance
                     GNPAs (Gross Non-Performing Assets) + Loan Growth + ROE (Return on Equity) + Collection Efficiency in Downcycles
                    
                    
20. Retail
                     Same Store Sales Growth + Growth Rates
                    
                    
21. Steel Tubes
                     Value Added + EBITDA (Earnings Before Interest, Taxes, Depreciation, and Amortization) Per Tonne
                    
                    
22. Telecom
                    ARPU (Average Revenue Per User) + Market Share
                    
                    
23. Textile
                     Geographies + Market Leadership + Margins + Which Part Of The Value Chain One Operates In
                     
                    
24. Tyre
                     2W (Two-Wheeler) or PV (Passenger Vehicle) or CV (Commercial Vehicle) or OTH (Other) + Margins + Domestic vs Exports
                    
                    This streamlined approach ensures you have the essential variables needed for effective peer-to-peer analysis across different sectors. Happy analyzing!
